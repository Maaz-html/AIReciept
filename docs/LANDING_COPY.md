## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Is it free?
Yes. SpendSmart AI is a free tool provided by Credex to help startups manage their burn more effectively. We don't charge for the audit or the report.

### What tools does it cover?
We currently support the 8 most popular AI tools for startups: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf.

### How accurate is the pricing?
Our database is updated weekly with the latest list prices for all supported tools. However, if you have a custom enterprise contract, your actual costs may vary.

### Will you spam me?
Never. We only ask for your email if you want to save your audit results or book a consultation with the Credex team to help implement the savings.

### What is Credex?
Credex is the financial OS for AI startups. We help companies manage their compute credits, tool spending, and financial health in one unified dashboard.
