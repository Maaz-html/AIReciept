# Unit Economics — SpendSmart AI

## Value Hypothesis
SpendSmart AI is a lead generation engine for Credex. Its goal is to capture high-intent leads who are already spending significantly on AI and convert them into Credex customers.

## Lead Value Math
Assume the average Credex deal value is **$2,000** (equivalent to 6 months of managed credits or savings at a $40/mo per seat optimization).
- **Credex Margin**: 20%
- **LTV per Converted Customer**: $400

## Funnel Conversion Targets
| Stage | Conversion Rate | Count (per 100) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Audits Completed** | 100% | 100 |
| **Email Captured** (Lead) | 30% | 30 |
| **Consultation Booked** | 15% | 4.5 |
| **Customer Purchased** | 40% | 1.8 |

**Blended Conversion Rate (Audit → Customer)**: ~1.8%
**Revenue per Audit**: $1.8 \times \$400 = \$7.20$ (Expected Value)

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
Since our GTM strategy relies on $0 budget channels, the CAC is primarily the "Time Cost" of distribution:
- **Hacker News / Reddit Posting**: 2 hours / month (~$100 value)
- **Twitter Outreach**: 5 hours / week (~$1,000 value / month)
- **Total Monthly Time CAC**: ~$1,100

If we generate **150 leads/month** (from ~500 audits), our **CAC per lead** is **$7.33**.
Given the Expected Value of $7.20 per audit, the system is almost immediately profitable on a time-adjusted basis, and highly profitable if the "Unfair Channel" (Credex pipeline) scales.

## Path to $1M ARR
To reach $1M ARR through this channel, we need to generate $83,333 in monthly revenue.

| Metric | Monthly Requirement |
| :--- | :--- |
| **New Customers Needed** | 208 (at $400 LTV/mo) |
| **Audits Needed** | 11,555 |
| **Leads Needed** | 3,466 |

### Scaling Strategy to $1M
1. **Automation**: Replace manual Twitter/Reddit outreach with programmatic SEO and targeted LinkedIn ads once the funnel is proven.
2. **Expansion**: Add 10 more tools to the auditor (e.g., Midjourney, Jasper, Notion AI) to capture broader search intent.
3. **Product-Led Growth**: Allow teams to "Claim their Savings" by clicking a button that automatically initiates the Credex onboarding process.
